# Your direct booking site

Superfrej gives you a zero-commission booking site out of the box. Open **Booking site** in the sidebar to find your links:

- **Your site** (`/book`) lists every active rental with its "from" price.
- Each rental has its own page you can share directly.

## What guests see

On a rental page, guests pick dates and party size and click **Check availability & price**. Superfrej checks your calendar (reservations *and* blocked dates) and your stay rules, then shows the exact price breakdown — nights, discounts, cleaning fee, city tax — from the pricing you configured.

If everything fits, they enter their name and email and book instantly. The reservation lands in your calendar as a **direct** booking, the guest joins your guest book, and they see a confirmation page with their stay summary.

## Good to know

- Guests can never book over an existing stay or a blocked period.
- Your minimum and maximum stay rules are enforced for guests (only you can override them, when entering a reservation manually).
- Prices always come from your Pricing settings — guests can't tamper with the total.
- Online payment isn't wired up yet; the confirmation tells guests payment is settled with you directly.
