# Using the calendar

Open **Calendar** in the sidebar for a month view of all your properties at once — one row per rental, one column per day. Dark bars are reservations (click one to open it); hatched bars are dates you've blocked.

## Booking from the calendar

Click any free day: the new-reservation form opens with the rental and check-in date already filled in.

## Blocking dates

Use **Block dates** (top right) when a property shouldn't be bookable — repairs, your own holiday, anything. Pick the rental, the first blocked day and the first free day, plus an optional reason that shows on the calendar.

To remove a block, click its bar once ("Remove block?") and once more to confirm.

You can't block dates that already have a confirmed reservation, and guests can't be booked over a block — the calendar always tells the truth.

## Getting around

Use the arrows to move month by month, and **Today** to jump back. Weekends are shaded; today's column is highlighted.
