# Managing your rentals

A *rental* is one property guests can book — an apartment, a house, a chalet, or a single room. Start here: everything else in Superfrej builds on your rentals.

## Adding a rental

1. Open **Rentals** in the sidebar and click **New rental**.
2. Fill in the four short sections:
   - **Basics** — the name guests will see, the property type, and an optional description.
   - **Location** — street, city, postal code, country.
   - **Capacity** — how many guests it sleeps, plus bedrooms, beds and bathrooms.
   - **Stay rules** — your check-in window (earliest and latest arrival time), the check-out deadline, and the minimum/maximum stay length in nights.
3. Click **Create rental**.

If something needs attention, the form highlights the exact field with a short explanation — for example, a maximum stay shorter than the minimum.

## Finding and editing rentals

- Use the **search box** to filter by name or city.
- Use the **Active / Archived / All** toggle to switch views.
- Click any rental to open and edit it. Changes are saved when you click **Save changes**.

## Archiving a rental

When you stop managing a property, open it and click **Archive**. The rental disappears from your active list but its history stays intact, and you can bring it back anytime with **Restore**. There is no delete — that's deliberate, so past reservations and statistics are never orphaned.
