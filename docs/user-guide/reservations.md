# Recording reservations

Open **Reservations** in the sidebar to see every stay across your properties — upcoming, past or cancelled.

## Creating a reservation

1. Click **New reservation**.
2. Pick the rental and the stay dates, and set how many adults and children are coming.
3. Enter the guest's details. If you've hosted them before (same email), Superfrej recognizes them and updates their card instead of creating a duplicate.
4. Optionally add the total price and private notes, then click **Create reservation**.

Superfrej protects you from double-bookings: if the dates overlap another confirmed stay on the same rental, the form tells you immediately. Back-to-back stays are fine — one guest can check out the same morning another checks in.

If the stay is shorter or longer than the rules you set on the rental, the reservation is still saved but you get a clear heads-up.

## Cancelling and restoring

Open a reservation and click **Cancel stay** — the dates become available again. If plans change back, **Restore** re-confirms it, as long as nobody else took the dates in between.

## Your guest book

From the Reservations page, open the **guest book** to see everyone who has stayed with you, with their contact details and language. Guests are added automatically from reservations — no data entry needed.
