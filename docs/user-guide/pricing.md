# Setting your prices

Open a rental and click **Pricing** to control what a stay costs there.

## Rates & fees

- **Nightly price** — your regular rate.
- **Weekend price** — used for Friday and Saturday nights; leave blank to charge the nightly price all week.
- **Cleaning fee** — added once per stay.
- **City tax** — per guest, per night (common in France: *taxe de séjour*).
- **Weekly / monthly discounts** — automatic percentage off the accommodation (not the fees) for stays of 7+ or 28+ nights. The monthly discount wins when both apply.

## Seasons

Add a season when prices change for part of the year — ski season, summer, holidays. Give it a name, a date range and its own nightly (and optional weekend) price. During those dates the season's prices replace your regular ones. Seasons can't overlap, and the end date is the first night back at the regular rate.

## Where these prices are used

Everywhere. When creating a reservation, click **Suggest price** to fill the total from your configured pricing — with the breakdown (nights, discount, fees, tax) shown underneath. The direct-booking site and channel sync will use the very same calculation.
